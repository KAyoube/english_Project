Joaquim Silva - Cybersecurity Manager

Cybersecurity management can be described as everything an organisation does to protect AI from cyber attacks, intrusions, malware and various types of data breaches. All businesses and public bodies are vulnerable to cyber attacks, which are increasing in sophistication and number. The proper functioning of networks and the protection of sensitive data require constant monitoring and proper cybersecurity management.

The AI engineer can create programs to teach the machine to react in place of man on subjects where the data is numerous and reliable enough to be organised. It's just as important to analyse the data, human behaviour and business processes as it's to organise them into a coherent system that can be transmitted to the machine. This is what is known as 'machine learning'. Deep learning" is concerned with more complex and comprehensive processes, based on what are known as "neural networks". The quadruple training of the EPITA IA institute allows to approach complexity with a real scientific, technical, managerial and human training, because the AI Engineer, in all his actions, must keep the measure of man and the sense of ethics.
